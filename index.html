OOTY: A Journey to the Heart of Nature

Nestled in the verdant embrace of the Nilgiri Hills, Ooty, also known as Udhagamandalam, is a haven of natural splendor and tranquility. This enchanting hill station, often referred to as the Queen of Hill Stations," offers a picturesque escape from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. At an elevation of 2,240 meters above sea level, Ooty's cool, refreshing climate and breathtaking landscapes make it a year-round destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Whether you're seeking adventure in its verdant landscapes, peace and relaxation in its serene environment, or a taste of its rich cultural heritage, Ooty promises an unforgettable experience. It's a place where every moment is a celebration of nature's bounty, and every vista is a postcard-perfect memory waiting to be cherished. Come, lose yourself in the magic of Ooty, and find a piece of paradise.

LOCATION

Ooty is a town and municipality in the Nilgiris district of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu. It is located in the Nilgiri Hills at an altitude of 2,240 meters (7,350 feet) above sea level. Ooty is approximately 86 kilometers (53 miles) northwest of Coimbatore and about 128 kilometers (80 miles) south of Mysore. The geographic coordinates of Ooty are approximately 11.4064° N latitude and 76.6932° E longitude.
